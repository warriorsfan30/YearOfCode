About

Hi! My name is Anish Kasam, and I am on my journey to learn coding. I want to learn how to code, since it is a vital skill.

I am making one program each day for a year, to further enhance my understanding of coding.

Projects will be posted at around 6:00 PM PST. I live on the West Coast in the US and I have other things such as school to attend to.
